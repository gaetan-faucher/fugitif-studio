    CONTEXT

    Virgil reclaims the sting. From a plant often feared, a new fabric is born—raw, resistant, and poetic. This circular brand reshapes perception, turning nettles into symbols of resilience, and clothing into statements. Between softness and sting lies a new kind of elegance.

    DIRECTION

    The identity draws on contrast: typographic spikes, fluid grids, muted greens. Materials echo tension—organic yet structured. Alternative punctuation becomes a visual rebellion, letting wearers express emotion instinctively, like wild weeds pushing through marble cracks.
