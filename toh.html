    TOM VAN DEIJNEN

    CONTEXT

    Tom van Deijnen, textile repair artisan and specialist in visible mending, based in Brighton (UK), is opening a namesake boutique-workshop. This physical space aims to expand his practice, attract new commissions, and reinforce his position as a credible, recognized maker.

    DIRECTION

    The identity embraces imperfection as a mark of care. Each element carries the trace of time. Frayed textures, disrupted grids, and worn down typography. Rather than hide the process, the design exposes it, inviting the viewer to look closer, read slower, and appreciate the quiet craft behind every stitch.
