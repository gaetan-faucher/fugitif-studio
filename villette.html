    PARC DE LA VILLETTE

    LA VILLETTE LOVES

    TO LEARN MORE

    The Festival of Cinema en Plein Air of La Villette has been a fixture of the Parisian summer for many years. For its 32nd edition, organized from July 15 to August 21, 2022, a new visual identity was essential to celebrate both the festival's heritage and its contemporary ambitions. The aim was to establish a strong connection between the event and the park's identity, while captivating a broad, family-friendly and youthful audience. The project also aimed to convey the social and cultural values of the festival, which combines cinema, sharing and nature in a unique outdoor experience.

    At the core of this identity, a graphic system centered on organic curves and wavy stripes. Symbolizing the breath of the wind, these shapes are a poetic evocation of the great outdoors, echoing a psychedelic aesthetic that conveys the values of community and reconnection with the living. This graphic choice also reflects the habits of spectators, remembering the picnic tablecloths that used to dot the lawns before each screening. This visual wink strengthens the emotional bond between the festival and its participants, while anchoring the identity in a convivial, shared universe.

    The logotype, designed with this same dynamic in mind, embodies these graphic elements to represent a space of conviviality and reconnection with nature. The use of wavy stripes, at the heart of the icon, offers remarkable flexibility, making it easy to adapt to a variety of communication media. This graphic system also adds visual dynamism by playing on perspectives and multiple reading points, increasing the impact and vitality of each application.

    To ensure a clear link with La Villette's visual identity, the emblematic red of the park was adopted as the central color. This monochromatic choice reinforces the coherence between the event and the parent identity, while adding a bold, contemporary touch. Every medium, whether posters, signage or digital content, is thus part of a visual harmony that speaks with a single voice.

    Spectators, whether novices or seasoned cinephiles, can enjoy a unifying experience, where cinema once again becomes a living, collective spectacle.
