    Solar Flow

    CAMPAGNE
    PUBLICITAIRE

    CONSOMMATION / MODE DE VIE

    SAYWATT

    CONTEXT

    In a world where energy feels distant and technical, Solar Flow brings power back home. Saywatt introduces a flexible solar panel for apartment living, transforming everyday windows into quiet sources of autonomy—flowing with light, movement, and possibility.

    DIRECTION

    A visual energy stream weaves through urban interiors, bending with walls, frames, and habits. The campaign embraces volume and depth to echo Solar Flow’s modular nature. The design makes domestic space feel alive—lit by flexibility, shaped by empowerment.
