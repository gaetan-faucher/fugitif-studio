    Robot LAMA

    CARTEL

    ARTS / CULTURE

    MUSÉE DES ARTS ET MÉTIERS

    CONTEXT

    At the Musée des Arts et Métiers in Paris, a new display invites young and curious minds to discover LAMA, a robotic explorer. Set against imagined Martian landscapes, the robot emerges—silent, precise, mysterious—prompting questions about space, signals, and survival.

    DIRECTION

    A white backdrop isolates LAMA, enhancing legibility while contrasting Mars’ dusty hues. Ochre and blue accents nod to the planet’s red soil and blue sunsets. A grainy overlay evokes signal noise, rooting the visual language in narrative realism and interplanetary tension.
