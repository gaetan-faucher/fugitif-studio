    ARTS / CULTURE

    MUSÉUM NATIONAL D'HISTOIRE NATURELLE

    CONTEXT

    The Muséum National d’Histoire Naturelle in Paris aims to release a collection of educational kits, each dedicated to a specific insect family or subcategory. These collectible boxes are designed to inform and spark curiosity, encouraging new perceptions and practices toward biodiversity.

    DIRECTION

    A visual identity for a minimum of three high-value collectible kits. Each box highlight a unique insect group while creating a cohesive ensemble. The identity elevate natural science into desirable, accessible, and instructive objects—bridging knowledge and emotional engagement with the living world.
