    CONTEXT

    Born from a collaboration with RO Studio, Le Jeu des 6 Vacances reimagines the classic family card game through screen printing and natural inks. Each family embodies a world. Among them, Ulysses sails—his aquatic journey tinted deep blue with mineral echoes of myth.

    DIRECTION

    A chromatic hierarchy guides the visual system. After testing vegetal and mineral pigments, lapis lazuli emerged as the ideal ink for Ulysses—evoking depth, salt, and odyssey. Each hue serves both narrative and material logic, fusing craft with meaning.
